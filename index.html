About Me

By night, I am the guitarist for Austrian metal-band Heathen Foray. By day, I am an Senior IT-Consultant and -Trainer. Which means, I cannot only shred a killer solo, I also know how to close Vim! I have been making music for more than 25 years, have released 6 studio albums and played 100+ shows. I am proudly endorsed by Solar Guitars and DR Strings. Sometimes, I also make videos. You can find some of my projects in the portfolio section.
